Main Coon cat

The Maine Coon is one of the largest domesticated breeds of cat. It has a distinctive physical appearance and valuable hunting skills.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Origin: United States of America
  • Average Length: 3.3 ft.
  • Mass: 13-18 lb (Adulte male)

The Maine Coon is a large and sociable cat, hence its nickname, "the gentle giant." It is characterized by a prominent ruff along its chest, robust bone structure, triangular body shape, an uneven two layered coat with longer guard hairs with a silky satin under layer undercoat , and a long, bushy tail.
